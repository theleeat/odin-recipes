Creamy Plant-Based Cucumber Salad

The Only Cucumber Salad You'll Ever Crave

This cucumber salad recipe is basically foolproof! It is so simple and easy! Making a cucumber salad really only involves a few things, cucumbers, red onions, and vegan creamy dressing.
