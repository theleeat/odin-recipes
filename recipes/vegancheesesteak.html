Vegan Cheese Steak

The Most Scrumptious Vegan Cheesesteak Recipe

My trick to making really delicious vegan food is to cook it and season it exactly like you would real meat. This means using the same methods and the same spices and seasoning. This vegan Philly cheesesteak recipe is basically foolproof! It is so simple and easy! Making a vegan cheesesteak really only involves a few things, cooked veggies, cooked meat, cheese, and bread. Of course, spiced to perfection with yummy seasonings!

Ingredients

  • Shitake, Baby Portabella, and White Mushrooms
  • Green Bell Peppers
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • American cheese
  • Mayo
  • Sub buns
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and green peppers.
  2. Slice the mushrooms.
  3. Brown the 'shrooms, onions and bell peppers in a frying pan over med heat.
  4. Season the veggies while cooking.
  5. Brown the inside of the buns in the oven, or face down on a skillet with vegan butter.
  6. Top the bun with your favorite sauce and cheese
  7. Add your veggies and enjoy!
